THE BEST COFFEE!

There is coffee that can change your day, and then there is
coffee that can change your life. Weaver's Coffee & Tea is
passionate about great coffee and social responsibility. We
hand-roast the best coffee and donate to three organizations: Dr.
Susan Love Research Foundation, Black Girls Code, and SF AIDS
Foundation.

We hand-roast 100% Arabica coffee beans from along the
equatorial belt, where the climate produces the most flavorful
coffee cherries. We support Fair Trade Certified, which means
that the farmers who grow our coffee beans are paid a fair price
for their crops. Order your coffee today and taste the difference!

We offer fresh coffee, hand-roasted daily and
available in whole bean or pre-ground. We
roast our coffee to order and package within
minutes of roasting, which ensures the coffee
arrives incredibly fresh. We offer thirty-two
delicious coffees. Order your coffee today.

Weaver's Coffee & Tea is a proprietary brand of Wild Card Roasters, LLC.

Our coffee roastery and corporate headquarters are located in San Rafael, California. We maintain sales offices in California, Japan, and Hawaii. Wild Card Roasters is an artisan coffee and tea company providing corporate solutions in sourcing, wholesale, retail, private label, licensing, e-commerce, training and consulting for specialty coffee and tea programs around the world. Wild Card Roasters owns and operates the retail brands Weaver’s Coffee & Tea and Harm’s Way Coffee Company.
